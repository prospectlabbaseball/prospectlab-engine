# Prospect Lab Score v1.0

## Purpose

The Prospect Lab Score (PLS) measures how likely a pitching prospect is to increase in dynasty fantasy baseball value over the next 6–12 months.

It is NOT a ranking of the best pitching prospects.

It is a ranking of expected future dynasty value growth.

---

# Prospect Lab Score Components

## 1. Performance

Measures how well the pitcher is performing.

Examples:

- Strikeout Rate
- Walk Rate
- K-BB%
- Swinging Strike Rate
- WHIP
- FIP
- xFIP
- Last 5 Starts

---

## 2. Stuff

Measures raw pitch quality.

Examples:

- Fastball Velocity
- Velocity Trend
- Stuff+
- Whiff Rate
- Chase Rate
- New Pitch Development

---

## 3. Development

Measures whether the player is improving.

Examples:

- Command Trend
- Velocity Trend
- New Pitch
- Workload
- Mechanical Changes
- Health Improvements

---

## 4. Opportunity

Measures opportunity to gain MLB value.

Examples:

- Current Level
- 40-Man Roster
- Rotation Openings
- Organization Promotion History
- Injury Openings

---

## 5. Scouting

Traditional scouting information.

Examples:

- Future Value
- Arsenal Grades
- Command Grades
- Physical Projection

---

## 6. Risk

Subtracts points.

Examples:

- Injury Risk
- Command Risk
- Reliever Risk
- Workload Limits
- Velocity Loss

---

# Outputs

The Prospect Lab Score powers:

- Weekly Top 100
- Weekly Top 20
- Trade Delta
- Buy / Hold / Sell
- Daily Movers
- Featured Player
- Newsletter
