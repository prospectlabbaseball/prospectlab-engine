# Prospect Lab Baseball

## Vision

Prospect Lab Baseball helps dynasty fantasy baseball managers identify breakout pitching prospects before consensus rankings catch up.

## Mission

Deliver the most accurate, data-driven pitching prospect intelligence in baseball.

## Core Principles

- Data over hype.
- Predictive analysis over reaction.
- Transparency in methodology.
- Continuous improvement.
- Every report must help dynasty managers make better decisions.

## Version 1 Goals

- Daily Pitching Prospect Rankings
- Prospect Lab Breakout Score
- Trade Delta
- Promotion Probability
- Daily X Posts
- Daily Newsletter

## Long-Term Vision

Become the most trusted source for predictive dynasty baseball intelligence.
