# Prospect Lab Engine v1.0

## Mission

Every morning, identify the pitchers whose dynasty fantasy baseball value changed the most in the last 24 hours.

The engine exists to answer one question:

"If I could only make one pitching move today, what should it be?"

---

## The Engine Processes

Every morning the engine will:

1. Collect previous day's MiLB statistics
2. Collect previous day's MLB statistics
3. Collect transactions
4. Collect injury news
5. Collect pitch quality changes
6. Collect velocity changes
7. Collect promotion and roster news
8. Update every tracked pitcher
9. Generate Prospect Lab recommendations

---

## Daily Questions

The engine answers:

Who should I add?

Who should I trade for?

Who should I trade away?

Who is closest to a promotion?

Who became significantly more valuable?

Who became significantly less valuable?

Who should I watch tonight?

---

## Primary Outputs

Strong Buy

Buy

Hold

Watch

Sell

Strong Sell

---

## Guiding Principle

Prospect Lab is not trying to predict who will have the best career.

Prospect Lab predicts who is most likely to gain dynasty fantasy value before the market recognizes it.
